Texas Commission for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing

The Texas Commission for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing (TCDHH) became part of the Department of Assistive and Rehabilitative Services on March 1, 2004. The services formerly provided through TCDHH are now provided through DARS. Visit the new website at http://www.dars.state.tx.us

Please change your bookmark to the new DARS site.

For questions or concerns, please call DARS at 1-800-628-5115 (Voice/TDD).
