People With Disabilities and Texas Business — A Profitable Partnership

The Texas Department of Assistive and Rehabilitative Services has expanded its focus to provide services to the state’s business community. As a result, people with disabilities, who have a higher unemployment rate than people with no disability, have more opportunities for employment. At the same time, businesses have access to free services that can help them lower recruiting and training costs, take advantage of tax incentives and benefits, and reduce turnover. | More about DARS business services.
