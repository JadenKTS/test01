Welcome to the

Texas Department of Assistive and Rehabilitative Services (DARS)

Four state agencies have merged into the new Texas Department of Assistive and Rehabilitative Services (DARS), which will provide programs and support for people with disabilities and families of children with development delays. “This new organization brings together four state agencies in a new partnership to help better meet the needs of the Texans we serve,” DARS Commissioner Terry Murphy said. “We have one of the best service delivery systems in the country, and our goal is to make it even better.”
